Filipino Giniling

Description:
Filipino Giniling is a savory and comforting dish made from ground pork or beef cooked with vegetables. It's a classic Filipino recipe that's easy to prepare and a favorite among Filipino families. Here's a description and a simple recipe for Filipino Giniling:

Recipe for Filipino Giniling:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground pork or beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground pork or beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the potatoes and carrots, and cook until slightly tender.
  4. Add the red and green bell peppers and continue to cook for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. Add the frozen green peas and cook until heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy your homemade Filipino Giniling, a hearty and flavorful dish that's perfect for sharing with family and friends!

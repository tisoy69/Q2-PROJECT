Caldereta

Description:
Caldereta is a classic Filipino dish known for its rich and hearty flavors. It's a stew made with tender chunks of meat (usually beef or goat) and vegetables, simmered in a savory tomato-based sauce. The dish gets its depth of flavor from a combination of ingredients such as liver spread, cheese, and olives. Caldereta is often enjoyed on special occasions and family gatherings.

Recipe for Caldereta:

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat (or goat meat), cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can liver spread (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (Eden or cheddar)
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pot and saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  2. Add the beef (or goat meat) and brown it on all sides.
  3. Stir in the liver spread and grated cheese until well combined.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and water, then let it simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours or until the meat is tender.
  5. Add the potatoes, carrots, red and green bell peppers, and green olives. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Enjoy the savory and cheesy flavors of homemade Caldereta, a Filipino favorite!

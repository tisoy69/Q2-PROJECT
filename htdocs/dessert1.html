Special Halo-Halo

Description:
Special Halo-Halo is a famous Filipino dessert known for its delightful mix of ingredients. This icy treat features a colorful combination of ingredients like crushed ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, beans, and leche flan, all topped with a scoop of creamy ube (purple yam) or langka (jackfruit) ice cream. It's a cool and refreshing dessert enjoyed by people of all ages, especially during hot days.

Recipe for Special Halo-Halo:

Ingredients:

  • Crushed ice
  • Sweetened fruits (bananas, sweet potatoes, jackfruit, etc.)
  • Sweetened sago (tapioca pearls)
  • Sweetened red munggo (red bean)
  • Sweetened white beans
  • Leche flan (caramel custard)
  • Purple yam (ube) or langka (jackfruit) ice cream
  • Shredded coconut
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl or glass, start by layering crushed ice at the bottom.
  2. Add sweetened fruits, sweetened sago, sweetened red munggo, and sweetened white beans on top of the ice.
  3. Place a slice of leche flan on the ingredients.
  4. Top with a scoop of purple yam (ube) or langka (jackfruit) ice cream.
  5. Sprinkle shredded coconut over the ice cream.
  6. Drizzle evaporated milk and add sugar to taste.
  7. Mix everything together and enjoy your homemade Special Halo-Halo!

Cool down with the delicious and colorful Special Halo-Halo, a popular Filipino dessert!

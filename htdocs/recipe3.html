Filipino Pork Menudo

Filipino Pork Menudo is a hearty and flavorful stew made with pork, liver, vegetables, and a tomato-based sauce. It's a beloved dish in Filipino cuisine. Here's a description and step-by-step guide to cooking Pork Menudo:

Ingredients:

- 1 pound pork, cut into small cubes

- 1/2 cup pork liver, cut into small cubes (optional)

- 1 small onion, chopped

- 3 cloves garlic, minced

- 2 cups tomato sauce

- 1 small potato, diced

- 1 small carrot, diced

- 1 red bell pepper, diced

- 1 green bell pepper, diced

- 1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)

- 1/2 cup raisins

- 2 hotdogs (Filipino-style sausage), sliced into rounds (optional)

- 2 tablespoons cooking oil

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 1 cup water

- Fish sauce or soy sauce (for seasoning)

Instructions:

1. Sauté the aromatics: In a large pan or pot, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions become translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

2. Add the pork and liver: Add the cubed pork (and liver if you're using it) to the pan. Cook until the meat turns brown.

3. Pour in tomato sauce: Pour in the tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine. Let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pork becomes tender and the sauce thickens. You may need to add more water if it gets too dry.

4. Add the vegetables: Add the diced potatoes, carrots, red and green bell peppers, and green peas. Continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked and tender.

5. Include the hotdogs and raisins: If using hotdogs, add them to the pot and stir. Simmer for a few more minutes.

6. Season with salt and pepper: Season the Pork Menudo with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add fish sauce or soy sauce for extra flavor, but be cautious as these can be salty.

7. Serve: Once the dish is cooked to your liking, remove it from heat. Pork Menudo is traditionally served with steamed rice and is often garnished with a few slices of hard-boiled eggs.

8. Enjoy: Pork Menudo is a delightful and hearty Filipino dish that's perfect for family gatherings and special occasions. It's known for its rich tomato-based sauce and a combination of sweet and savory flavors.

Pansit Palabok

Description:
Pansit Palabok is a popular Filipino noodle dish known for its vibrant orange sauce and delicious toppings. The dish features rice noodles smothered in a rich and savory shrimp sauce, and it's typically garnished with various toppings like shrimp, crushed chicharr√≥n (crispy pork skin), hard-boiled eggs, and green onions. It's a delightful and flavorful dish that's often served on special occasions.

Recipe for Pansit Palabok:

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces rice noodles (bihon)
  • 1/2 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup ground pork
  • 1/2 cup shrimp broth
  • 2 tablespoons annatto powder (achuete)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup tinapa (smoked fish), flaked
  • 1/2 cup chicharr√≥n, crushed
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Lemon or calamansi wedges

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rice noodles (bihon) according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat cooking oil and saut√© ground pork until brown.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink. Set aside some cooked shrimp for garnish.
  4. Add shrimp broth, annatto powder, fish sauce, and water. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Add the cooked rice noodles and mix them with the sauce.
  6. Transfer the Pansit Palabok to a serving platter.
  7. Top with shrimp, tinapa flakes, crushed chicharr√≥n, hard-boiled eggs, and green onions.
  8. Serve with lemon or calamansi wedges.

Enjoy the vibrant flavors of homemade Pansit Palabok, a classic Filipino noodle dish!
